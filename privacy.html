Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

AnkleTap is built by Wonder Vision Labs Ltd, a UK limited company. This policy explains what information AnkleTap collects, why, and how it's handled.

Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers. Full stop. AnkleTap has no analytics or tracking SDKs beyond what's needed to run the app and process subscriptions.

What we collect

Voice recordings. When you record a voice note, AnkleTap transcribes it using Apple's on-device Speech framework. On supported devices, this happens entirely on your iPhone — the audio never leaves your device. Where on-device transcription isn't available, the recording is processed by Apple's own speech recognition service, governed by Apple's privacy policy. AnkleTap itself does not send your voice recordings to any third-party transcription service.

Task and ritual content. Task titles and your answers to the onboarding "anchor" questions are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate ritual suggestions and your personal anchor statement. This includes the text of what you type or say — for example, a task title, or your answers about what helps you when you're stuck. No name, email address, or device identifier is included in these requests. Anthropic processes this text to generate a response and does not use it to train their models.

Sign-in information. AnkleTap supports Sign in with Apple and Google Sign In. When you use either, the identity provider (Apple or Google) shares a user identifier and, depending on your settings, an email address with the app. This is stored securely on your device (in the iOS Keychain) to keep you signed in. We do not separately collect a password or store it ourselves.

Microphone access. AnkleTap requests microphone access so you can record voice notes for tasks, logbook entries, and anchor questions. You can decline this and type instead — voice input is never required.

Subscription and purchase data. Subscriptions are handled entirely by RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. AnkleTap does not see or store your payment details. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier tied to your subscription status — not your name, email, or any other personal identifier.

What we don't collect

  • We do not use location tracking. AnkleTap does not request or access your device's location.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

Where your data lives

Your tasks, rituals, logbook entries, and completion history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not synced to any AnkleTap server. If you delete the app, this data is removed from your device. Voice recordings used for ritual generation or anchor statements are processed in the moment and not retained by AnkleTap after transcription.

Your controls

  • Export your data. From Settings, you can export your saved tasks, completions, and logbook entries as a file.
  • Delete your data. From Settings, you can permanently delete all locally stored data and sign out.
  • Microphone permission. You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device's Settings app — AnkleTap will fall back to typed input.
  • Sign out. Signing out clears your locally stored credentials and identity tokens.

Children

AnkleTap is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of AnkleTap after a change means you accept the updated policy.
